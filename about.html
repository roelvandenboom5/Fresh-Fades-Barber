Our Story
More than a barbershop
How a passion for precision fades became Malta's most-loved barbershop.
The Beginning
From one chair to two shops

Fresh Fades started in 2019 with a simple belief: every person deserves to walk out of a barbershop feeling like the best version of themselves. What began as a single chair in Swieqi quickly grew into something bigger — a community of barbers and clients who share a passion for quality.

Founded by a team of dedicated barbers, Fresh Fades was built on the idea that a haircut isn't just a service — it's an experience. From the moment you walk in, you're greeted with good music, great conversation, and the confidence that you're in expert hands.

"We don't just cut hair. We build confidence. Every fade, every line, every detail — it's personal."

In 2024, Fresh Fades was awarded Best Barber Salon in Malta — a recognition of the team's relentless pursuit of perfection. That same year, the second location opened in Sliema, bringing the Fresh Fades experience to even more clients across the island.

Today, with 10+ skilled barbers across two locations, Fresh Fades continues to raise the bar. Every barber on the team is handpicked for their skill, creativity, and dedication to the craft. From classic cuts to bold designs, skin fades to beard sculpting — the team does it all, and does it exceptionally.
