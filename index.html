Questions
Frequently asked
Do I need to book an appointment?
Walk-ins are welcome at both our Swieqi and Sliema locations, but we recommend booking online to guarantee your preferred time slot and barber. You can book 24/7 through our website.
How long does a haircut take?
A standard haircut takes about 30–40 minutes. More detailed services like hair designs or the full grooming package (haircut + beard + facial) can take up to 60–75 minutes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and contactless payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay at both locations.
Can I choose my barber?
Absolutely! When booking online, you can select your preferred barber at either location. Each barber has their own speciality — check out their profiles on our Barbers page to find your perfect match.
What are your opening hours?
Swieqi: Mon–Thu 9:00–20:00, Fri 8:00–20:00, Sat 9:00–19:00, Sun closed. Sliema: Mon–Sat 10:00–20:00, Sun closed. We recommend booking for Friday and Saturday as these are our busiest days.
Do you offer services for kids?
Yes! We offer kids' haircuts (under 10) starting from €10 at both locations. Our barbers are experienced with children and will make sure they have a great experience.
What's the cancellation policy?
We kindly ask you to cancel or reschedule at least 2 hours before your appointment. You can do this easily through your booking confirmation email or by contacting us via WhatsApp.
